The short version

Every business is telling a story. Most of them are telling it badly.

I started in communications and innovation at the Met Office, then spent a decade building businesses, directing films and developing intellectual property of my own. That work converged into CharacterX, a methodology rooted in cinematic storytelling that treats the customer as the protagonist and the brand as the guide, and into NextMonth, the platform I am building to put that method to work.
